AI Creator Jam

AI Creator Jam is an immersive, hands-on workshop that invites participants to explore the growing intersection between art, design, and artificial intelligence. The session introduces a range of generative AI tools that make it easier than ever to bring creative ideas to life. Whether you have a background in design or you’re completely new to the field, this workshop will show you how to use cutting-edge AI platforms to turn words into visuals, ideas into images, and inspiration into polished poster concepts.

Participants will learn how to craft effective prompts for AI models, refine generated content, and combine multiple tools to produce cohesive visual designs. The workshop emphasizes creativity, experimentation, and collaboration, encouraging attendees to play with different styles, color palettes, and design directions. With step-by-step guidance, participants will gain confidence in navigating popular AI design tools and discover techniques for enhancing their creative workflow.

AIdeathon 2025 - Chaos Edition

AIdeathon 2025 - Chaos Edition is a dynamic workshop designed to introduce participants to the LUMA Design Thinking framework, a structured yet flexible approach to creative problem-solving. Through guided activities and interactive discussions, participants will learn how to approach challenges with empathy, generate innovative ideas, and develop solutions that are both practical and impactful. The session provides an accessible introduction to the key stages of the LUMA framework—looking, understanding, and making—while emphasizing collaboration and experimentation as core elements of the design thinking process.

Participants will then put their learning into action by applying the LUMA methods to real-world scenarios. Working in teams, they’ll analyze user needs, brainstorm creative solutions, and prototype their ideas in an engaging, hands-on environment. By the end of the workshop, attendees will have not only experienced the power of design thinking firsthand but also gained practical tools and mindsets they can use to solve complex problems in their studies, workplaces, and communities.

Q-Team Showcase

Department X Sharing is a showcase session where the department presents its latest progress, research, and initiatives in the fields of Generative AI and Robotics. The session highlights ongoing projects, breakthroughs, and collaborations that demonstrate how emerging technologies are shaping the future of automation, creativity, and intelligent systems. Participants will gain insights into how Gen AI and robotics are being integrated into real-world applications, from creative media and human–machine interaction to engineering and design innovation.

The sharing aims to inspire curiosity and spark discussion about the possibilities of next-generation technology. Attendees will have the opportunity to learn directly from the teams behind these developments, gaining a deeper understanding of the department’s vision and approach to interdisciplinary innovation. Whether you’re an enthusiast, student, or researcher, Department X Sharing offers a glimpse into how the department is driving forward the boundaries of AI and robotics in education and research.
